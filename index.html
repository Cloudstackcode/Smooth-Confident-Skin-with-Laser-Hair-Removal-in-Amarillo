Frequently Asked Questions

Does laser hair removal hurt?

Most people describe the sensation as a mild snap against the skin—similar to a rubber band. Discomfort is minimal, and the laser’s cooling system helps reduce it even more.

How many sessions will I need?

Typically, 6 to 8 sessions are needed for optimal results, but this varies from person to person. We’ll give you a clear plan during your consultation.

Is it safe for all skin types?

Yes, we use advanced laser systems that are safe and effective for a wide range of skin tones and hair types. We tailor settings to suit your specific profile.

What areas can be treated?

We can treat nearly any part of the body, including the face, legs, arms, underarms, bikini line, chest, and back.

Is there downtime?

Most clients resume normal activities right after treatment. Some redness or mild swelling may occur, but it typically subsides within a few hours.
